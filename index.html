Andrea and I first met in 7th grade in our pre-teens, but we did not become friends right away. We were actually mortal enemies for all of 7th grade until we both forgot why we hated each over summer vacation and became best friends in 8th grade (ahh teenage angst). We were roommates for 5 fun-filled years right out of college, when we were both just starting out in our professional careers and learning how to be real grown-ups. I had the privilege of being her Maid of Honor at her wedding, and now she is doing me the honor of being mine. I am so excited to share this special day with her!

Andie and I first in first grade when we were 6 and were Brownies together. I consider her my mature friend, and am very happy I will have her to turn to for marriage advice, as she has been happily married for 13 years. We have been friends for 30 years (man we’re old)! We always joke that she is the old lady of our group, but she still knows how to cut loose and have a great time for the right occasion!

I met Connie about 8 years ago when she started dating my good college friend Roark, and we have become quite close over the years over countless game nights that quickly turn into nail painting nights (much the the boys’ chagrin), shopping excursions, and girls’ trips. I am now lucky to call her one of my closest friends, and am so happy she agreed to be a bridesmaid, even though she will be a new mom with a 5 month old baby girl!

I also met Malia in first grade when we were 6 and Brownies together. Despite living miles apart and our busy schedules, we always manage to squeeze in a quick phone call to catch up (usually when we’re both in traffic!) Even though we’re now in our mid-thirties, I still feel like a kid when we get together, and I love the history we have. I can’t wait to celebrate with her!

I met Nikki through Dur about 10 years ago, and since moving back up to the Bay Area, we have had the opportunity to spend more time together and gotten to be good friends. She always knows how to make me laugh and I enjoy our girls nights out. We’ve made some great memories together in Hawaii, Vegas, Napa, and Monterey. I am so honored to have her in the wedding party!
